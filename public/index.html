01 RepOne artifact one

One intelligent interface for the field — cutting tool chaos and turning data into action.

RepOne is a universal rep interface that unifies today's fragmented pharma field technology landscape into a single, intelligent experience powered by Agentic AI. Built for field reps and commercial teams, it replaces constant tool switching across CRM, analytics, content, and operations with one curated view and one clear next-best action per context. Unlike traditional dashboards or point solutions, RepOne acts as a forever frontend that adapts as systems change — reducing cognitive load while increasing field effectiveness.

what it does
Consolidates 15+ disconnected systems into one interface, curates signals across data sources, and delivers a single prioritized next-best action — so reps focus on HCP engagement, not admin.
who it's for
Field reps and commercial organizations managing complex CRM, data, content, and compliance ecosystems at scale.
why now
Reps are overwhelmed by rising data volume, constant tool changes, and competing recommendations that erode trust. Agentic AI makes it possible to move from dashboards and alerts to real decision orchestration.
field experience part of the future-of-field thinking
02 FieldOS artifact two

The operating system for field effectiveness — turning field intelligence into journey progression and Rx uplift.

FieldOS solves the "realization problem": the gap between prescriptions written and patients who actually stay on therapy. It shifts the focus from activity to impact by operationalizing journey progression and removing barriers across roles. What makes it different is that it converts frontline signals into coordinated action and continuous improvement — not more disconnected tactics.

what it does
Captures field interactions, observations, and barriers as frontline signals — then turns them into structured, cross-team interventions and learning loops.
who it's for
Commercial and field enablement leaders who need impact, not just activity. Cross-functional field-facing teams (sales, MSLs, access, patient services) coordinating instead of working in silos.
why now
For every 100 prescriptions written, only 29 patients remain on therapy at 12 months. Today's siloed roles surface friction too late — and more training, tech, or promotion doesn't fix realization.
field operations part of the future-of-field thinking

Customer Experience is evolving — let's discuss and see where we can co-create together.

bet one → pre-diagnosis · advocacy · patient journey

Trust before the prescription.

The average narcolepsy patient waits over a decade for a diagnosis. The pre-diagnosis journey is where trust in a pharma brand could start being built — long before there's anything to prescribe.

why it's a Jazz bet → Jazz already has long-standing relationships with the advocacy organisations that could partner on improving the pre-prescription patient journey. The infrastructure to build trust early is closer than it looks.

bet two → platform · patient relationship · build vs partner

Direct-to-patient infrastructure.

A DTP platform could be a promising option for rare disease patients, who are geographically dispersed and navigating access pathways that weren't designed for their complexity. The mid-sized pharma question: build, partner, or fall behind.

what's already happening → Eli Lilly (LillyDirect) and Pfizer (PfizerForAll) have made direct bets on owning the patient relationship end-to-end. Jazz's current experiment portfolio could be a way of exploring this option without a full platform commitment.

bet three → commercial model · field roles · HCP engagement

Quality over quantity of engagement.

Some leading pharma companies are moving away from frequency-based commercial models toward fewer, smarter, more contextually relevant interactions. The bet: deeper interactions outperform more interactions.

what's already happening → Bayer UK consolidated five customer-facing field roles into three. Early signal that the industry is willing to take real headcount risk in exchange for more holistic, meaningful customer interactions.
