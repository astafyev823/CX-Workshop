10:15 — 11:15 · what's next · CX support through 2026

The road ahead.

Four areas of focus plus an open card for discussion. Let's align on what you need to be set up for success.

VMO-wide
Workstream · Flexible Support

On-demand VMO Support

Provide flexible, on-demand capacity to the VMO to advance priority CX Transformation initiatives. Includes support for KPI/success-measure alignment, exec narratives, CX engagement moments (e.g., roadshows/hackathons/connection days), and bringing the customer voice into brand planning inputs.

What's new New/expanded "capacity buffer": less pre-scoped deliverables, more responsive support that can flex with shifting priorities.
Tiffany
Workstream · Field Operating Model

Future Field Operating Model, Phases 1A—3

Enable the definition of a future-facing US Field Operating Model through structured phases: Phase 1A current-state inventory; Phase 1B "from–to" role synthesis + role clarity framework; Phase 2 integrated workflow & customer experience view; Phase 3 leadership decision & implications pack. Built via templates, facilitation, structured comparisons, and synthesis.

What's new More structured, phased approach with explicit leadership decision points; emphasis on integration/workflows.
Workstream Leads
Workstream · Experiment Support

Targeted Enablement of Experiments

Hands-on support to designated workstream leads as needed to maintain momentum and quality across experiments.

What's new Enabling leads with structure, cadence, and leadership-ready synthesis for their experiments.
VMO-wide
Workstream · AI-Driven Experimentation

AI-Driven Experimentation Support Agent

Define, design, and initially enable an AI-driven experimentation support agent. Focus is on usability and alignment to CX governance/success measures.

What's new Moves from manual tracking/synthesis toward an AI-enabled operating assist.
